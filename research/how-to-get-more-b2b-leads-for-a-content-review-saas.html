Executive Summary

To get more B2B leads for a content review SaaS like Filestage, the most effective strategy is to implement a multi-faceted growth framework focused on four key pillars: Content Marketing & SEO to build organic authority and capture high-intent search traffic; Paid Acquisition on search and social channels to generate immediate, qualified leads; Strategic Partnerships & Integrations with complementary tools like project management and DAM platforms to tap into new audiences; and Product-Led Growth tactics, including an optimized free trial and in-app referral loops, to convert users into paying customers and drive viral growth.

Target Markets

  • • Mid-market and enterprise teams
  • • Creative agencies
  • • Regulated industries (pharma, finance)
  • • North America & Europe focus

Core Advantages

  • • User-friendly interface
  • • Transparent pricing
  • • Multi-format support
  • • Unlimited reviewers
