Revenue Calculation Breakdown

Calculation Formula

1. Monthly Clicks: Volume × 4% CTR
2. Annual Clicks: Monthly × 12 months
3. Conversion: 0.5% × 5% = 0.025%
4. Customers: Annual clicks × 0.025%
5. Revenue: Customers × $8,000

Example Calculation

Keyword: "best interview questions" (1,900 volume)
Monthly Clicks: 1,900 × 4% = 76
Annual Clicks: 76 × 12 = 912
Customers: 912 × 0.025% = 0.228
Annual Revenue: 0.228 × $8,000 = ~$1,824
